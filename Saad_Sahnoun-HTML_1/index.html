The Intouchables

Directors: Olivier Nakache & Éric Toledano

Genre: Drama Comedy

Year Released: 2011

Awards: NAACP Image Awards
Fun Fact: Based on a true story

A story about the unlikely friendship that develops between a wealthy quadriplegic man, Philippe, and his ex-convict caretaker, Driss. The unlikely pair forms a bond as Driss brings a refreshing energy to Phillipe's monotonous life. Both men from opposite ends of the social spectrum find common ground and positively impact each other's lives.
